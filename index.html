About.

With over 10 years of experience, we specialize in residential service so you can be certain that your home will be respected and cleaning will be done safely and efficiently. We do not use any cleaning solutions with harsh chemicals and our soap does not leave any residual odor. In addition to the glass, we also clean screens. We unfortunately do not disassemble and clean storm windows.

At this time, we only perform work on Ground Floor or 1st story windows. Anything that requires a ladder to reach is NOT considered Ground Floor work.

Service.

Home owners often overlook windows when performing routine cleaning. Clean windows make a big difference in the overall atmosphere of your home. Keeping your windows clean also helps the glass stay in good shape. You want to take care of your windows because replacing them can be very expensive.

Washing your windows with a rag and over-the-counter cleanser won't cut it. Instead of doing the work yourself, hire a professional to save you time and effort while getting the best result possible.

Contact.

Estimates can typically be done with your address provided and a quick look at Google Earth. In your message please include:
-your address OR
-# of windows (counting in panes is preferred i.e. 1 window with 2 sliding pieces and 1 fixed piece is 3 panes).
-whether you want a price for inside & outside, just the outside cleaned, or both.
-any other details you think we should know.

All estimate prices include cleaning screens.
